Bitcoin Pouch is...

Easy

No download or installation required. Just sign up and you can start using bitcoins right away!

Quick

Because the bitcoins in your Bitcoin Pouch have been pre-verified, you don't have to wait for a miner to confirm any transactions between users. Just type the username of the member you want to send coins to and you're ready to go.

Free

No added transaction fees here! Bitcoin Pouch is entirely supported by user donations and the efforts of our volunteers.

Mobile

Bitcoin Pouch also features the easiest-to-use cross-platform mobile interface. Sending and receiving bitcoins is as easy as pulling out your cellphone and firing up Bitcoin Pouch.

Open Source

No nasty surprises, no proprietary code, no secrets. Bitcoin Pouch is committed to creating an open financial system held accountable to the people it serves. Part of that means making the source code of our website available for anyone to examine or modify. (You can find it on GitHub, if you're curious.)

In the spirit of full disclosure alluded to in the paragraph above, Bitcoin Pouch is presently running on:

  • Ubuntu 10.10
  • Django 1.0
  • Bitcoin 0.3.21
  • Apache 2.2.16
  • MySQL 14.14
